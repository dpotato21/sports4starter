NBA 2021 Playoffs

The 2021 NBA playoffs have officially began and with fans slowly being allowed back into the stadiums for the postseason, it has been nothing short of special. As can be seen in the image, these are the matchups for the first round:

  • Utah Jazz VS Memphis Grizzlies
  • Los Angeles Clippers VS Dallas Mavericks
  • Denver Nuggets VS Portland Trail Blazers
  • Phoenix Suns VS Los Angeles Lakers
  • Philadelphia 76ers VS Washington Wizards
  • New York Knicks VS Atlanta Hawks
  • Milwaukee Bucks VS Miama Heat
  • Brooklyn Nets VS Boston Celtics

These matchups were determined through the regular season and seeding rules. Along with the Play-In Tournament, where the bottom seeds in each conference played an elimination style tournament to determine who gets the 7th and 8th seed. Through this tournament, the Golden State Warriors, San Antonio Spurs, Indiana Pacers, and Charlotee Hornets were eliminated. The Milwaukee Bucks and Miami Heat have actually finished their series, as the Bucks swept the Heat in 4 games. So they will wait to see who else advances as the rest of the series are yet to be determined.

Mark Eaton



Recently was the passing of Mark eaton. Mark played the center position for 11 years with the Utah Jazz. He was 64 years old and retired from the NBA in 1993 as an NBA All-Star, 2 time NBA Defensive Player of the Year, 3 time NBA All-Defensive Team and 2 time NBA All-Defensive Second Team. Once he retired the Utah Jazz retired his jerysey number as well, No.53.
