Free Agency News:

Aaron Rodgers

Recently, superstar Green Bay quarterback Aaron Rodgers has made public his frustrations with the organizations. Reports are that he wants out of the team, however he himself has not come out and confirmed anything. Rodgers did NOT show up for OTA's along with the team's top receivers. Many believe that he would like to be traded to the Denver Broncos or the Las Vegas Raiders. Packers general manager Brian Gutekunst among others within the organization have come and specifically said they will NOT be trading Aaron Rodgers, and that they expect to come to an agreement sooner than later. There has been no new news.

Julio Jones

Another large piece of news this offseason, the All-Pro wide receiver Julio Jones is reported to be traded from the Atlanta Falcons. Atlanta was reportedly trying to ship Julio before and during the Draft but were asking for too high a price. On a recent episode of the sports talk show "Skip and Shannon Undisputed," Jones himself broke the news that he was "Outta there," in reference to returning with Atlanta. This has led fans to wonder what the new asking price for the receiver is, now that Atlanta's leverage has been ruined. Whatever the price may actually be, it has not stopped fans from photshopping the receiver in their favorite team's jersey. No news has broken yet on a trade.
